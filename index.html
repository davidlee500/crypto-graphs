Crypto Market Performance Analysis

About This Chart

This interactive chart shows the average performance of Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and the rest of the crypto market (TOTAL3) following significant market drops.

Specifically, it tracks:

  • Performance over 90 days after market drops of >10%
  • Data from the past year
  • Percentage change from the drop point
